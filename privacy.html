Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Overview

Orion Studio is a desktop application designed to help creators generate, organize and publish original video content to their own social media accounts.

Information We Collect

  • TikTok OAuth authentication tokens.
  • Basic account information authorized by the user.
  • Publication metadata required to upload videos.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Passwords.
  • Private messages.
  • Followers lists.
  • Personal conversations.
  • Personal files unrelated to Orion Studio.

How We Use Information

Information is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate the user.
  • Publish videos approved by the user.
  • Display connected account information.
  • Maintain publishing sessions.

Data Storage

Authentication credentials are stored locally whenever possible. Orion Studio does not sell, rent or share user information.

User Control

Users may disconnect their accounts at any time. Users remain in control of every publication.

Third-Party Services

Orion Studio integrates with official APIs provided by platforms such as TikTok. Their services are governed by their own Terms and Privacy Policies.
